Introduction

In order to analyze the words that are used in this project, Affective Norms for English Words (ANEW) , which provides a set of normative emotional ratings for words in the English language. This set of verbal materials have been rated in terms of pleasure, arousal, and dominance.

In this project the data that is being analyzed is taken from twitter, and you can see it by clicking the username in the "Tweets" section. The objective is to determine what is the affect of the person based in the text in the tweet. And in real life application it could help to determine if a person has a suicide tendency and prevent a disaster.

K-means

The K means clustering method is used in order to determine the number and types of emotions being detected.

The model used for the affect classification is the developed by Russell and Feldman Barrett, the structure core affect in the model can be described by two independent dimensions.

  • The degree of pleasantness
  • The degree of activation
The pleasure or displeasure can be described as good - bad mood, positive - negative, pleasure - pain. The relation or simialrity between this types summarizes how well one is doing. The activation refers to a sense of mobilization or energy. The same relation as sleep through drowsiness, alertness, relaxation, hyperactive, frenetic. The activation rises and falls during the day depending one's own physical activity, and the events of the day. The distribution and the result of the graph resulting by the combination of pleasure and activation, represents the distribution of different possible affects, but the center of the space is an neutral point, which can not tell the affect, so it was considered as a "Unknown" affect.

Now, analyzing the model. A positive affect consists of a feeling that vary in activation, as the same way in negative affects. High activation consist in a feeling that vary in pleasure. The project does not take into consideration of bipolarity or the detection of severe high and low moods and changes in energy, thinking and behavior, based on the text, and it brings false positives. So, it is assume that a neurological reaction has a psychological event which can be represented as a positive and negative reaction or stimulus.
